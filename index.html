MISSION STATEMENT

At Minority Program Connector, we seek to provide guidance and connect people to activities they set out to accomplish.



ABOUT US

Getting yourself engaged in an activity is great, but without commitment can lead to someone giving up only to repeat the process later on. We here at Minority Program connector give people the channel in which to find information on programs in their area in order to develop skills. Currently focusing on children in school, summer programs are engaging and fun. Using idle time away from school productively.
